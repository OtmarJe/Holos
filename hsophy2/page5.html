Holosophycally speaking ...

Social change, economic change, political change, structural change - the world is on the move. Today already feels like the day before yesterday.

Trying to shape the future with yesterday's thinking ensures tomorrow's mistakes.

Relationship is the magic word of the 21st century. Relationship creates value. This applies to partnership. And to work.

Rigid structures dissolve. The shriveled branches are swept from the trees. Only what bends will not break.

Take the risk to show yourself. But remember: If you show your innermost, express your desires and present your talents, you need a protective space to feel safe.

The more distributed the power of decision, the less the impetus to gain a position of superiority for oneself.

The age of information is a time of empowerment. The empowerment of the self is what philosophers have been dreaming of for centuries.

Core decision makers are overwhelmed by today's complexity. The intelligent swarm is increasingly needed in decision-making.

In large organizations, management is decoupled from its employees. Middle management tries to close this gap - and is overburdened by the rapid changes in production conditions.

Holistic governance acts as servant leadership, relies on the competence of employees and strengthens their decision-making power. This is effective, efficient and sustainable.

Today's society needs quick answers, quick solutions. The hierarchical decision-making dinosaurs of the past are overwhelmed by the complex challenges of the present.

Large organizations are collapsing these days because they can't handle the gradual transition to horizontal decision making. They would have to reinvent themselves.

If you love your work, your work will turn into a lovable product. Buyers and users will love it. The world needs less and less unlovable and therefore unloved products.

Self-empowerment and the sharing of responsibility characterize the progress of the present. Self-empowerment and strategies for withdrawing as well as shunning responsibility are increasingly outdated.

Shared responsibility relies on communication. Meaningful exchange is the fuel of change. However, if too many channels are used simultaneously, a fog of meaning is created. Agile methods such as Scrum help to clear the fog.

Holosophy means a kind of cybernetic cooperation. There are strong and weak interactions. Weak are disinterest, lack of motivation and hierarchy conflicts. Strong is combined enthusiasm.
