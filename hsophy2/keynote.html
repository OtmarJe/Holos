The world is on the move. Feeling accepted and safe will give us a foothold. Then we can appreciate the strengths of the others and deal truthfully with our weaknesses. The result is honesty towards ourselves and others. Honesty with regard to our desires. Honesty in dealing with our dreams and goals. This is Holosophy – a tool to support creativity, to expand your box and to find a stable position when solid grounds seem to sway.
