The world is on the move. Feeling accepted and safe will give us a foothold. Then we can appreciate the strengths of others and deal truthfully with our weaknesses. The result is honesty towards ourselves and others. Honesty with regard to our desires. Honesty in dealing with our dreams and goals. This is Holosophy – a stable perspective when solid ground seems to sway.
